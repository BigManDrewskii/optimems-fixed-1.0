At Optimems, we're building the future of energy management — and we need your help to make it beautiful, intuitive, and powerful.

We're seeking a Frontend Developer to join our team and craft exceptional user experiences for our growing platform.

## About the Role

This is a full-time hybrid role based in **Thessaloniki**, where you'll shape how users interact with our energy management systems.

You'll be responsible for developing user-facing features that make complex energy data simple and actionable. Your work will directly impact how businesses manage their renewable energy assets.

## What You'll Do

**User Interface Development**
- Build responsive, accessible interfaces using modern frameworks
- Implement designs that transform complex data into clear, actionable insights
- Create reusable components that maintain consistency across our platform
- Optimize applications for maximum speed and scalability

**Collaboration**
- Work with designers to transform UI/UX concepts into pixel-perfect implementations
- Partner with backend engineers to integrate APIs and data streams
- Contribute to design system discussions and best practices

**Technical Excellence**
- Ensure technical feasibility of designs before implementation
- Implement proper state management for optimal performance
- Write clean, maintainable code that follows industry standards

---

## What You Bring

**Development Skills**
- Strong **Front-End Development** expertise with modern frameworks
- Experience in **Responsive Web Design** and mobile-first approach
- Solid understanding of **Web Development** and **Software Development** principles
- Knowledge of **Back-End Web Development** for seamless integration

**Problem-Solving**
- Strong analytical skills and attention to detail
- Creative approach to technical challenges
- Ability to optimize performance while maintaining code quality

**Collaboration**
- Excellent communication skills for working with cross-functional teams
- Team-oriented mindset with eagerness to share knowledge
- Ability to give and receive constructive feedback

**Technical Foundation**
- Experience with **Agile methodologies** is a plus
- Understanding of web performance optimization techniques
- **Bachelor's degree** in Computer Science or related field
