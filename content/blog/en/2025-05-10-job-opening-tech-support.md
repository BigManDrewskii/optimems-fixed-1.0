At Optimems, we're transforming how energy is managed across renewable energy plants.

We're seeking a Technical Support specialist to help deploy our innovative solutions and support our growing customer base.

## About the Role

As a Technical Support specialist, you'll be the bridge between our cutting-edge technology and real-world RES plant operations. You'll work directly with clients to ensure their energy management systems run smoothly.

This is a hybrid role based in **Thessaloniki**, combining remote support with occasional on-site visits to RES plants across Greece.

---

## What You'll Do

Your day-to-day responsibilities will include:

**Client Support**
- Provide technical guidance to clients on **+SolarControl** system setup and operation
- Perform remote diagnostics to troubleshoot hardware and software issues
- Monitor system performance to ensure optimal operation
- Document solutions to continuously improve our knowledge base

**Field Work**
- Participate in on-site installations at RES parks (mostly day trips)
- Travel to client locations for hands-on support
- Build relationships with plant operators and technical teams

**Collaboration**
- Work closely with our development team to identify and resolve product issues
- Partner with the sales team to enhance the customer experience
- Contribute to the improvement of our support workflows

---

## What You Bring

We're looking for someone who combines technical expertise with strong communication skills.

**Technical Foundation**
- **Technical Degree** in Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, or related field
- Basic understanding of **networking (IP, routers, VPNs)** and Linux-based systems
- Familiarity with industrial environments and RES plants

**Problem-Solving Skills**
- Strong analytical thinking and a methodical approach to troubleshooting
- Ability to diagnose complex issues remotely
- Resourceful and adaptable when facing new challenges

**Collaborative Mindset**
- Eagerness to learn and grow technically
- Strong communication skills for explaining technical concepts
- Team-oriented approach to problem-solving

**Flexibility**
- Willingness to travel occasionally for on-site support
- Adaptability to work across different client environments

## Why Join Optimems?

- **Innovative Technology**: Work on cutting-edge energy management systems that are transforming the renewable energy landscape
- **Growth Opportunities**: Learn from experienced engineers and grow your technical skills in a fast-paced startup environment
- **Impact**: Your work directly helps RES plant operators optimize their energy production and reduce costs
- **Collaborative Culture**: Join a team that values knowledge sharing, mutual support, and continuous improvement
- **Hybrid Flexibility**: Enjoy the balance of remote work with occasional field visits to interesting locations

## Bonus Skills

While not required, experience in these areas would set you apart:

- Experience with **SCADA**, **Modbus**, or telemetry/monitoring systems
- Familiarity with **MQTT brokers** or **Raspberry Pi**
- Basic programming/scripting knowledge (Python, Bash, etc.)
